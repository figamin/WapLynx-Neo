Net Friends

Here's a list of sites that are considered friends of wapchan. They're all worth visiting.

Descriptions were either taken from the sites themselves or written by their admins.

Current Friends

These sites are safe for work, close in tone to wapchan itself, and have taken the formal step of crosslinking with us, signifying friendship.


  • hikari3.ch is a relaxed imageboard with user created boards.
  • SheepishPatio is a web forum for hobbies, their users like books, games and anime among other things.
  • nashikouen is a fun place, with pears!

Sites I like

These sites aren't friends in the sense of crosslinking, but I personally think they're neat.


  • Sushichan is a great little relaxed imageboard
  • UY & Chill is one of the few good sources of English UY discussion.
  • The Post Office is pretty unique in being split into a pure textboard with no images and a pure imageboard with no text.

Legacy NSFW

These sites were added when I was a bit looser with friend guidelines. They're fine, but you're probably gonna see hentai, so consider this your warning.


  • heyuri is a BBS with a Futaba style imageboard, a 2channel style textboard, an oekaki board, a western Ayashii World copy, and cool users.

  • Mootxico is an imageboard born of honest fun which rejects the current ironic culture of the Internet and instead aims for a more traditional, oldschool approach.

  • [prog] on ƒçudan (textboard) is a programming textboard.

The Graveyard

These sites were friends with wapchan but are now down. Here's to the memories:
